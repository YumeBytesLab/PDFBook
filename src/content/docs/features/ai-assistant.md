PDFBook includes an optional **AI assistant** that can help you understand what you're
reading. It's a panel you open from the top bar (or with <kbd>Ctrl</kbd>/<kbd>⌘</kbd> +
<kbd>T</kbd>), and it's aware of the page currently on screen.

## Bring your own provider

The assistant connects to an AI provider of your choice, so you stay in control of where
your data goes and what it costs. Supported providers include:

- **Ollama** — run a model locally on your own machine.
- **OpenAI**
- **Claude (Anthropic)**
- **OpenRouter**

Add your provider and model in **Settings → AI**. If you use a local model with Ollama,
nothing leaves your computer.

## What it can do

- Answer questions about the page or spread you're viewing.
- Summarize or explain passages.
- Help you find your way around a long document.

## Privacy

The assistant is entirely optional and off until you configure a provider. When you use a
cloud provider, the text you ask about is sent to that provider under your own account; a
local Ollama model keeps everything on your device.
