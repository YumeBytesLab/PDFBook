## Everything your PDFs need, in one app

PDFBook is a desktop application for people who keep a lot of PDFs — books, papers,
manuals, manga, scanned documents — and want one calm place to read and manage them.
It runs entirely on your computer: your files never leave your machine.

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Distraction-free reader" icon="open-book">
    Page-flip and vertical-scroll modes, right-to-left support for manga, in-document
    text search, bookmarks, and reading-position memory that picks up where you left off.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Organized library" icon="list-format">
    Tags, ratings, favorites, reading status, and watched folders that keep your
    catalog in sync as you add files.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Create & extract" icon="document">
    Build PDFs from images or pages, capture pages from another app on screen, and
    pull pages back out when you need them.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Private by design" icon="approve-check">
    No account required to read. Everything is processed locally; your library never
    leaves your device.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Get going in two minutes

1. [Download](/download/) the installer for your platform.
2. Add a folder of PDFs as a watched path.
3. Open a book and start reading.
